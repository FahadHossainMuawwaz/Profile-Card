FC Barcelona

La Liga Club

FC Barcelona is one of the most famous football clubs in the world, based in Spain. Founded in 1899, the club is known for its motto “Més que un club,” meaning “More than a club.” FC Barcelona is famous for its attacking style of play and strong youth academy, La Masia. The team has won many domestic and international trophies, including La Liga and the UEFA Champions League. Legendary players like Lionel Messi have played for the club. FC Barcelona has millions of fans worldwide.
